About Us

MUDRIKA FINANCE Your Banking Partner

Mudrika Finance is an innovative and new age start-up for Digital loan, insurance, and Bank account opening services on our online platform we will implement ML/AI to find credit worthiness, to provide them with suitable amount of loan and at the same time we will involve technology for customer profiling which will help them to choose the insurance plan which will fit best for their relevant need and comfort.

  • Will elevate financial literacy among the EWS ,LIG and MIG section of the society along with our business growth in the market.
  • Will implement ML/AI to find credit worthiness, to provide them with suitable amount of loan
  • Develop our own underwriting Early warning systems, which will help financial institutions in better collections of the money
  • Will have our own rule engine in place which will consider various other factors like Bank Statement, ITR, KYC details and SMSs
  • Initial credit underwriting of a person, which is just not dependent on the CIBIL score provided by Credit bureaus
